About Zotte Lotte

Zotte Lotte takes care for a children's party from the beginning to the end.
That means that you can hire her for not only children's party but for other fun child activities as well. She is also available for entertainment at a wedding or for face painting.

ONE THING IS SURE: it is always a party with Zotte Lotte


Watch this video to get the party mood going!

No longer worries about your child's birthday party!

Zotte Lotte takes care of everything from the beginning of the party until the very end. She prepares the parties and is the entertainment as well as the supervisor. There are multiple locations available in the neighborhood of Hilvarenbeek (which is near Tilburg), but a party at home is of course a possibility as well! Do you live in Noord-Brabant? Then Zotte Lotte would love to come by! She offers various theme parties with associated activities. Besides children's parties Zotte Lotte loves to face paint as well. She also arranges other activities for children during events or weddings. Everything is possible! Just contact and discuss your whishes. As long as you remember:

Life is just one big party!
