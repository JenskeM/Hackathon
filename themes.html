Zotte Lotte has a various of theme parties ready to go!
She has made a difference between the parties for children from 4 until 7 and from 8 until 12 years old. She already prepared these parties especially for you. At the time of the party, Zotte Lotte loves to be present when the gifts are given (and maybe get something tasty as well). Afterwards she will start her program. You don't have to prepare anything: Zotte Lotte takes care of all of it! So your caretakers can just sit back and relax. Zotte Lotte will attend the party for 3 hours (whatever themed party it is).
So which party will it be?

Theme parties 4 until 7 years old

Are you between the 4 and 7 years old then Zotte Lotte has prepared the following theme parties for you.

Aye Aye - Party

Are you brave and completely fearless? Aren't you afraid of water? And can you set up a great 'ARGH'? Yes?
Then you have found the treasury with this party!
During this party Zotte Lotte is going to test if you can be a real pirate. She herself dares everything, but does the same counts for you? Do you dare to become a real pirate?
Then yell: AYE AYE, CAPTAIN!

Princesses - Party

Is pink your favorite color? Do you like to wear a pretty dress? But most important: do you dream to be a princess? With this party your dream will come true! You and your friends get a complete make-over and become true princesses (or princes if you prefer). You don't need a fairy godmother to have your own amazing FAIRY TALE. Your party will definitely have a happy ending.

Baking - Party

Do you like eating cookies? Zotte Lotte LOVES it!
She prefer to eat at least 10 cookies a day. Especially when they are made herself... Hmm when they are so fresh and warm. DELICIOUS! You can bake these amazing cookies as well, together with Zotte Lotte and your friends. Of course, all these cookies must me tasted as well and Zotte Lotte can eat a lot, but not THAT much. So you and your friends have to help Zotte Lotte with tasting them. But are you all truly bakers? That we have to test of course as well.

Farm - Party

Is a farm your favorite spot? And aren't you afraid to become a bit dirty? (Don't let your parents read that...)
In this party you will visit a REAL FARM. On a farm is of course pretty big, so enough space to enjoy a lot of different games with your friends. But what is the most fun part of a farm? Exactly, the animals! So to make your party even more awesome, you and your friends may help the farmer with feeding the cows! How fun is that?! So put on your boots and get ready to become a farmer for a day.

Circus - Party

Do you enjoy to give a show? Are you a real performer? Then a dream comes true when becoming a circus artist! Not only will there be magicians, but acrobats and clowns as well. There will be even more circus artists! But what is a circus without a circus director? No, that's not really possible. So of course, a circus director will present the entire show! Zotte Lotte herself will be behind the camera. So you can review your amazing show as much as you want with all of your friends and family. It will be magical!

Superhero - Party

Every child has a real superhero in him or herself. Do you want to not only become a superhero on the inside, but on the outside as well? Zotte Lotte can make that possible! What is a superhero without his mask or cape? Together you will create your own! But of course, a superhero has to have some qualities. Therefore it will be tested if you are a good superhero or not. But those tests will be very easy for you right? Or will those tests be like kryptonite for you? Choose this party and like a superhero Zotte Lotte ZOOOFFS right to you!

Theme parties 8 until 12 years old

Are you between the 8 and 12 years old then Zotte Lotte has prepared the following theme parties for you.

Movie star - Party

On social media you see a lot of video clips. In most of them a real celebrity is present as the star. But what if YOU become the STAR? That would be amazing right?! Together with your friends you will make a lipdub and all of you will be real stars! Of course, not everybody who likes video clips likes to be in the spotlight as well. If that sounds like you, then the role of director will be perfect for you.
So, Camera ready? Set? ACTION!

Lab - Party

Are your parents sometimes afraid that you will set the house on fire since you are experimenting again? Or do you love to watch or make something transform into something complete different? Is making slime with your friends a dream coming true? Then choose this party! During this party you will perform experiments together with Zotte Lotte. Everybody may even take their own experiment with you all so that of the parents can enjoy it as well. Put on your lab glasses and be ready for a BOOMING party!

Detective - Party

Are you always the one who finds the easter eggs first?
Do you always spot the differences between two pictures? Then you are a real detective and Zotte Lotte needs your help! Unfortunately something awful happened and Zotte Lotte can't solve it herself. So could you and your friends please help her solve the mystery?
Of course is this party only for REAL geniuses. But you probably already knew that...

Army - Party

Do you consider yourself a tough person? And do you consider yourself sportive and fit? Have you ever wondered what it will be like to be in the army? This is your chance to proof that you are indeed as tough and sportive as you think. By completing the various assignments during the party you can show whether you can become a real soldier! Put on your camouflage cloths and your brave-face.
Zotte Lotte would love to SALUTE you at the sports venue B-fit in Hilvarenbeek.

Escape - Party

Zotte Lotte has a not only an awesome car and great outfit, she has her own escape-room as well! That is pretty cool right?! Are you a master in puzzling? Do you and your friends work together like a team? And also important: are you fast? This is all necessary if you want to escape out of the room made by Zotte Lotte. Of course she thinks that you can't. Time to proof her wrong! You can prove yourself to her at 't Podium in Hilvarenbeek.

Vlog - Party

Zotte Lotte loves to just lay in bed and watch YouTube video's all day. Does that sounds familiar? Wouldn't it be fun to be in a video yourself? Hopefully you aren't afraid of the camera, want you will be the one who's vlogging this time! During the vlog-party you and your friends have to execute a various amount of challenges. Zotte Lotte will film these challenges. So you and your friends can review your own vlog-video's as often as you want (and of course while you're in bed). So, put on a pretty smile and the video will be definitely amazing with you as the STAR!
