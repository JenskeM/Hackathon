Friends book

Zotte Lotte has been to a lot of parties already. In all those parties she have made a lot of new friends. But Zotte Lotte is sometimes a bit forgetful (she's getting old), so she can't remember every child she has been to. That's why Zotte Lotte has come up with a solution: she has made a friends book for herself! Do you know what a friends book is? Maybe you have one yourself, but in a friends book all of your friends are answering some questions about themselves and add something fun for the owner of the book. She answers all those questions herself as well. So you all get to know a bit more about Zotte Lotte too! If you have pictures made during the party with Zotte Lotte, she would love to see them as well! You can upload them here as well. But before you upload these pictures, please ask your mommy or daddy if it is alright that these pictures are shown on social media. Of course Zotte Lotte checks everything before she puts it online.

So, do you want to write in Zotte Lotte's friends book?
